About Me

A journey from coding passion to data-driven problem-solving.

Hello! I'm Romit Jain, a graduate student pursuing an MS in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence at The University of Texas at Dallas, with a strong passion for data science, predictive modeling, and analytics to drive business impact.

Proficient in Python, SQL, R, I have experience with Tableau, Power BI, Excel, Google Cloud, and Machine Learning. As a Business Analyst Intern at AVIK, I managed the full data analysis life cycle for business development, analyzing 75+ data points weekly, integrating datasets with SQL, and facilitating strategic discussions with senior leadership. Previously, as a Data Analyst Intern at THE ZEAL, I enhanced data integrity, automated validation processes, and developed interactive dashboards that improved decision-making efficiency. My Personality Classification System project leveraged machine learning to analyze user profiles, achieving 90% classification accuracy and improving predictive capabilities.

Beyond academics, I am a passionate sports enthusiast, with experience competing in soccer and basketball tournaments, as well as esports competitions in FIFA. These experiences have honed my skills in strategic thinking, adaptability, and teamwork, which I apply both on and off the field.
